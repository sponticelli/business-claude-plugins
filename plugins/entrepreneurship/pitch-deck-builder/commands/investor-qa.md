# Investor Q&A Prep

Generate answers to the most common investor questions, tailored to your specific startup and stage.

## Usage

```
/investor-qa [describe your startup and current state]
```

## Process

1. Analyze your startup's profile
2. Identify likely question categories
3. Generate tailored Q&A pairs
4. Flag potential concerns and mitigation strategies
5. Provide follow-up preparation tips
