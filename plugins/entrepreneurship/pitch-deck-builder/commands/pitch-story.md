# Craft Pitch Story

Develop a compelling narrative framework that connects your startup's story from problem to solution to vision.

## Usage

```
/pitch-story [describe your startup and founder journey]
```

## Process

1. Identify the emotional core of your story
2. Map the narrative arc (problem → journey → solution → future)
3. Find the "why now" moment
4. Connect founder story to company mission
5. Create memorable hooks and sound bites

## Output Format

```markdown
# Pitch Narrative: [Company Name]

## The Core Story
[2-3 paragraph narrative that can be delivered in 60 seconds]

## Story Framework

### Opening Hook
[Attention-grabbing first line]

### The Problem World
[Paint the picture of life before your solution]

### The Turning Point
[What made you/market realize change was needed]

### The Solution Journey
[How you discovered/built the answer]

### The Transformation
[What changes with your solution]

### The Vision
[Where this leads - the big picture]

## Key Sound Bites

### One-Liner
"[Memorable description of what you do]"

### Problem Statement
"[Quotable problem framing]"

### Why Now
"[Timing justification]"

### Vision Statement
"[Inspiring future state]"

## Storytelling Tips

### Emotional Beats
1. [Beat 1: Hook/Curiosity]
2. [Beat 2: Problem/Pain]
3. [Beat 3: Hope/Solution]
4. [Beat 4: Proof/Traction]
5. [Beat 5: Vision/Inspiration]

### Personal Connection
[How to weave founder story authentically]

### Memorable Moments
[Specific anecdotes or data points to emphasize]
```

## Example

**Input**: `/pitch-story I quit my VP role at a Fortune 500 after spending 3 years frustrated with broken internal communication. Built a tool that saved my team 10 hours/week.`

**Output**: Narrative framework including:
- Hook: "I walked away from a $400K salary because I was tired of missing dinner with my kids due to pointless meetings"
- Problem world: Enterprise communication chaos
- Turning point: A missed birthday party due to an email chain
- etc.
