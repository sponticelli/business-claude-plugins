# Investor Targeting

Create a prioritized investor target list based on stage, sector, and check size fit.

## Usage

```
/investor-targeting [describe your company stage, sector, location, and funding needs]
```

## Reference Check Questions

### For Portfolio Founders
1. How involved is [Partner] post-investment?
2. How did they behave when things got tough?
3. Would you take their money again?
4. How helpful were they with intros/hiring?
5. Any surprises after the term sheet?

### Red Flags to Listen For
- Slow to respond/help
- Aggressive board behavior
- Changed terms late in process
- Poor follow-on support
