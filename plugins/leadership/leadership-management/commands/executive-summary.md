# Executive Summary

Create clear, impactful executive communications including board updates, stakeholder summaries, all-hands presentations, and crisis communications.

## Usage

```
/executive-summary [communication type and context]
```

Provide details about:
1. What type of communication (board update, investor update, all-hands, crisis)?
2. Who is the audience?
3. What is the key message or update?
4. What decisions or actions are needed?
5. What is the current situation/context?

## Process

1. **Clarify Audience and Purpose**
   - Who needs to know?
   - What do they care about?
   - What action is needed?

2. **Structure the Message**
   - Lead with the headline
   - Support with key points
   - Provide context as needed
   - Close with clear asks

3. **Anticipate Questions**
   - What will they ask?
   - What concerns will they have?
   - Prepare responses

4. **Polish for Impact**
   - Clear and concise
   - Data-supported
   - Action-oriented

## Example

**Input**: `/executive-summary Q4 board update for Series A SaaS company, grew 15% but missed target, need to discuss extending runway`

**Output**: Complete board update with:
- Executive summary with "Attention Needed" status
- Metrics dashboard showing 15% growth vs. 20% target
- Analysis of why target was missed
- Updated runway projections with scenarios
- Specific ask: approve revised budget or discuss bridge financing
- Clear decision timeline

---

Apply immediately to user's input. Ask clarifying questions about audience, key metrics, or specific concerns if needed.
