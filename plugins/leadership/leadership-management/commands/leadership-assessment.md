# Leadership Assessment

Comprehensive leadership assessment with competency evaluation, style analysis, and personalized development planning.

## Usage

```
/leadership-assessment [leader context and development goals]
```

Provide details about:
1. What is the leader's current role?
2. What leadership challenges are they facing?
3. What feedback have they received?
4. What are their career aspirations?
5. What specific areas need development?

## Process

1. **Assess Current State**
   - Leadership competencies
   - Leadership style
   - Strengths and gaps
   - Stakeholder feedback themes

2. **Identify Development Priorities**
   - Critical gaps for current role
   - Gaps for future aspirations
   - Quick wins vs. long-term development

3. **Create Development Plan**
   - Specific actions
   - Learning resources
   - Experience opportunities
   - Support structures

4. **Define Success Metrics**
   - Observable behaviors
   - Feedback indicators
   - Business outcomes

- Style analysis showing directive tendency, need for participative flexibility
- 360 feedback themes synthesized
- Development plan focused on stakeholder management and executive presence
- 6-month roadmap with executive coaching recommendation
- Clear success metrics tied to cross-functional effectiveness
