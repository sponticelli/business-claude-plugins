# Performance Review

Create comprehensive performance reviews with achievement assessment, competency evaluation, development planning, and goal setting.

## Usage

```
/performance-review [employee context and review period]
```

Provide details about:
1. What role is being reviewed?
2. What time period (quarter, half-year, annual)?
3. What were their goals/OKRs?
4. Key achievements and challenges?
5. Development areas to address?

## Process

1. **Assess Achievement**
   - Review goals and deliverables
   - Evaluate quality and impact
   - Consider context and challenges

2. **Evaluate Competencies**
   - Role-specific competencies
   - Company values/behaviors
   - Growth trajectory

3. **Identify Development Areas**
   - Skills to build
   - Behaviors to change
   - Experiences to gain

4. **Set Future Goals**
   - Performance expectations
   - Development objectives
   - Career alignment

## Example

**Input**: `/performance-review Annual review for Senior Software Engineer who shipped major features but struggled with cross-team communication`

**Output**: Complete performance review with:
- "Meets Expectations" rating with detailed rationale
- Goal achievement (3 of 4 goals met, 1 exceeded)
- Competency assessment showing technical strength, communication gap
- Development plan focused on stakeholder management
- Next year goals with communication-focused stretch goal
- Career discussion for Staff Engineer path

---

Apply immediately to user's input. Ask clarifying questions about specific goals, achievements, or concerns if critical information is missing.
