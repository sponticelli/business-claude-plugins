# IP Strategy

Develop comprehensive intellectual property strategy including identification, protection, and management of IP assets.

## Usage

```
/ip-strategy [company and IP context]
```

Provide details about:
1. What type of business/product do you have?
2. What IP assets might you have (technology, brand, content)?
3. What is your stage (startup, growth, established)?
4. What is your budget for IP protection?
5. Any specific IP concerns (competitors, infringement)?

## Process

1. **Identify IP Assets**
   - Patents and patentable inventions
   - Trademarks and brand assets
   - Copyrights and creative works
   - Trade secrets and confidential information

2. **Assess Protection Needs**
   - Value of each asset
   - Risk of loss/infringement
   - Competitive landscape
   - Budget constraints

3. **Develop Protection Strategy**
   - Registration priorities
   - Trade secret protocols
   - Contractual protections
   - Enforcement approach

4. **Create Management Plan**
   - Portfolio management
   - Monitoring
   - Ongoing protection
