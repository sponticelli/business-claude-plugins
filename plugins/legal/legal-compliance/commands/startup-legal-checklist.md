# Startup Legal Checklist

Generate a comprehensive, stage-appropriate legal checklist for startups including formation, founder agreements, IP protection, and compliance requirements.

## Usage

```
/startup-legal-checklist [company stage and context]
```

Provide details about:
1. What stage is your startup (idea, pre-seed, seed, Series A+)?
2. What industry/sector are you in?
3. Where are you incorporated/operating?
4. How many founders/employees?
5. Any specific legal concerns?

## Process

1. **Assess Current State**
   - Company stage
   - Industry requirements
   - Jurisdiction considerations
   - Team composition

2. **Identify Requirements**
   - Formation documents
   - Founder agreements
   - IP protection
   - Compliance needs
   - Employment matters

3. **Prioritize Actions**
   - Critical (do now)
   - Important (do soon)
   - Standard (plan for)

4. **Create Action Plan**
   - Specific documents needed
   - When to engage counsel
   - DIY vs. professional help

## When to Engage Legal Counsel

### Always Use a Lawyer For:
- [ ] Formation documents (if complex cap table or multiple founders)
- [ ] Fundraising (term sheet negotiation, closing documents)
- [ ] Major commercial contracts (enterprise deals, partnerships)
- [ ] Employment disputes or terminations
- [ ] IP disputes or infringement claims
- [ ] Regulatory compliance (industry-specific)

### Can Often DIY (with templates):
- [ ] Basic NDAs
- [ ] Simple contractor agreements
- [ ] Standard offer letters
- [ ] Basic privacy policy (with tools)
