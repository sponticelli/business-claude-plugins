# Risk Assessment

Comprehensive risk assessment covering legal, operational, financial, strategic, and compliance risks with mitigation strategies.

## Usage

```
/risk-assessment [business context and focus areas]
```

Provide details about:
1. What type of business do you have?
2. What is your industry/sector?
3. What stage are you (startup, growth, established)?
4. Any specific risk concerns?
5. What risk areas do you want to focus on?

## Process

1. **Identify Risks**
   - Legal risks
   - Operational risks
   - Financial risks
   - Strategic risks
   - Compliance risks
   - Reputational risks

2. **Assess Risks**
   - Likelihood
   - Impact
   - Current controls
   - Residual risk

3. **Prioritize Risks**
   - Risk scoring
   - Critical risks
   - Acceptable risks

4. **Develop Mitigation**
   - Control strategies
   - Transfer options (insurance)
   - Acceptance criteria
