## Button Copy Variations

### Value-Focused CTAs
| Current | Optimized | Why It Works |
|---------|-----------|--------------|
| Submit | Get My Free [Resource] | Emphasizes value received |
| Sign Up | Start My Free Trial | Shows what they get |
| Learn More | See How It Works | Specific next step |
| Buy Now | Get [Product] Now | Ownership language |
| Download | Get Instant Access | Speed + exclusivity |

### Recommended Button Copy Options

**Option 1 (Value)**:
> [Action Verb] + My/Your + [Specific Value]

**Option 2 (Benefit)**:
> [Start/Get/Unlock] + [Main Benefit]

**Option 3 (Urgency)**:
> [Claim/Reserve] + My/Your + [Offer] + [Urgency Word]

**Option 4 (Outcome)**:
> [Yes,] I Want + [Desired Outcome]

## Visual Optimization

### Button Design

**Color Recommendations**:
| Background | Industry | Notes |
|------------|----------|-------|
| Orange/Red | E-commerce | High urgency, action |
| Green | Finance, Health | Trust, go signal |
| Blue | SaaS, B2B | Professional, safe |
| Contrast | Any | Stand out from page |

**Size**: Large enough to tap on mobile, but not overwhelming
**Shape**: Rounded corners (friendlier) vs Square (more professional)
**Shadow/3D**: Subtle depth makes it look clickable

### Placement Recommendations
1. Above the fold (primary CTA)
2. After key benefit section
3. After social proof
4. At page bottom (final CTA)
5. Sticky header/footer (optional)
