# Headline Variations

Generate multiple headline options for A/B testing using proven copywriting formulas.

## Usage

```
/headline-variations [your product/service and the main benefit or transformation]
```

## Output Format

```markdown
# Headline Variations: [Product/Service]

## Context

**Product**: [What you're selling]
**Target Audience**: [Who you're targeting]
**Primary Benefit**: [Main value proposition]
**Current Headline** (if any): [Existing headline]

---

## Benefit-Focused Headlines

### Direct Benefit
1. "[Get/Achieve] [Specific Result] [Timeframe/Qualifier]"
2. "The [Adjective] Way to [Desired Outcome]"
3. "[Transform/Turn] Your [Current State] Into [Desired State]"

### Benefit + Differentiator
4. "[Achieve Result] Without [Common Pain/Sacrifice]"
5. "Finally: [Benefit] That [Key Differentiator]"
6. "The Only [Product Type] That [Unique Benefit]"

---

## Problem-Focused Headlines

### Pain Agitation
7. "Tired of [Pain Point]? [Solution]"
8. "Stop [Negative Activity] and Start [Positive Activity]"
9. "[Pain Point] Is Costing You [Loss]. Here's the Fix."

### Question Format
10. "Still [Struggling With Pain Point]?"
11. "What If [Desired Outcome] Was [Easier/Faster/Cheaper]?"
12. "Are You Making These [Number] [Topic] Mistakes?"

---

## Social Proof Headlines

### Numbers-Based
13. "[Number]+ [People/Companies] [Achieved Result] With [Product]"
14. "Join [Number] [People] Who Already [Achieved Benefit]"
15. "Rated #[Number] for [Category] by [Authority]"

### Testimonial-Based
16. "See How [Customer Type] [Achieved Specific Result]"
17. "'[Short Quote About Result]' — [Customer Name]"
18. "The [Product] That [Customer Type] Are Raving About"

---

## Curiosity Headlines

### Secret/Insider
19. "The [Secret/Hidden] [Thing] Behind [Desired Result]"
20. "What [Successful People] Know About [Topic] That You Don't"
21. "[Number] [Topic] Secrets [Authority] Won't Tell You"

### Discovery
22. "Discover the [Adjective] Way to [Achieve Result]"
23. "The [Result] Method [Audience] Is Talking About"
24. "Unlock [Benefit] in Just [Timeframe]"

---

## Urgency/Scarcity Headlines

### Time-Based
25. "[Achieve Result] in [Specific Timeframe]"
26. "Start [Benefiting] Today—Not [Longer Time]"
27. "[Time-Sensitive Offer]: [Benefit] Before [Deadline]"

### Limited
28. "Limited: [Offer] for [Specific Audience] Only"
29. "Only [Number] Spots Left for [Offer]"
30. "[Exclusive Access] to [Benefit]"

---

## How-To Headlines

### Process-Focused
31. "How to [Achieve Desired Result] in [Number] Steps"
32. "How [Customer Type] [Achieved Result] (And How You Can Too)"
33. "The Step-by-Step Guide to [Achieving Result]"

### Simplicity
34. "The Easiest Way to [Achieve Result]"
35. "[Achieve Result]—No [Common Requirement] Required"
36. "Get [Result] Without [Complicated Process]"

---

## Contrarian Headlines

### Myth-Busting
37. "Why [Common Belief] Is Wrong (And What to Do Instead)"
38. "Forget [Old Way]—Here's What Actually Works"
39. "[Counter-Intuitive Statement]: Why [New Approach] Wins"

### Bold Claims
40. "The End of [Old Problem/Way]"
41. "[Product] Isn't [Category]—It's [Better Category]"
42. "Warning: [Product] May [Positive Transformation]"
```
