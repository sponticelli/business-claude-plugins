# Growth Strategy

Develop a comprehensive growth strategy identifying opportunities, growth levers, and execution roadmap using proven frameworks.

## Usage

```
/growth-strategy [current state and growth objectives]
```

Provide details about:
1. What is your current business/product?
2. Current revenue/users/metrics?
3. What are your growth objectives?
4. What constraints exist (budget, team, time)?
5. What growth efforts have you tried?

## Process

1. **Assess Current State**
   - Current metrics and trajectory
   - Existing growth channels
   - Customer acquisition economics
   - Product-market fit signals

2. **Identify Growth Opportunities**
   - Market penetration
   - Market expansion
   - Product expansion
   - Diversification
   - (Ansoff Matrix analysis)

3. **Evaluate Growth Levers**
   - Acquisition channels
   - Activation improvements
   - Retention strategies
   - Revenue expansion
   - Referral programs

4. **Prioritize Initiatives**
   - Impact vs. effort scoring
   - Resource requirements
   - Risk assessment

5. **Build Execution Roadmap**
   - Phased approach
   - Milestones and metrics
   - Resource allocation
