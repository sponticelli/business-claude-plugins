# Competitive Analysis

Deep-dive competitive analysis using Porter's frameworks, positioning maps, and strategic benchmarking.

## Usage

```
/competitive-analysis [your company/product] vs [competitors or market]
```

Provide details about:
1. What is your company/product?
2. Who are your main competitors?
3. What market segment are you analyzing?
4. What strategic questions are you trying to answer?

## Process

1. **Identify Competitive Landscape**
   - Direct competitors
   - Indirect competitors
   - Potential new entrants
   - Substitute products/services

2. **Profile Key Competitors**
   - Company overview and positioning
   - Product/service offerings
   - Target market and segments
   - Pricing strategy
   - Strengths and weaknesses

3. **Analyze Industry Forces (Porter's Five Forces)**
   - Threat of new entrants
   - Bargaining power of suppliers
   - Bargaining power of buyers
   - Threat of substitutes
   - Competitive rivalry intensity

4. **Map Competitive Positioning**
   - Key differentiating dimensions
   - Position relative to competitors
   - White space opportunities
   - Segment attractiveness

5. **Develop Strategic Implications**
   - Competitive threats to address
   - Opportunities to exploit
   - Recommended strategic responses
