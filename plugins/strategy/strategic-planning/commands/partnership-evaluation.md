# Partnership Evaluation

Comprehensive evaluation of strategic partnerships, joint ventures, alliances, or M&A opportunities.

## Usage

```
/partnership-evaluation [partnership opportunity to evaluate]
```

Provide details about:
1. What is the partnership opportunity?
2. Who is the potential partner?
3. What are your strategic objectives?
4. What would each party contribute?
5. What are your concerns or questions?

## Process

1. **Strategic Fit Assessment**
   - Alignment with strategy
   - Complementary capabilities
   - Cultural compatibility
   - Vision alignment

2. **Value Creation Analysis**
   - Synergies identification
   - Revenue opportunities
   - Cost efficiencies
   - Strategic benefits

3. **Risk Evaluation**
   - Execution risks
   - Relationship risks
   - Market risks
   - Financial risks

4. **Deal Structure Consideration**
   - Partnership models
   - Governance structure
   - Economics and incentives
   - Exit provisions

5. **Recommendation**
   - Go/no-go decision
   - Negotiation priorities
   - Due diligence requirements
