# Market Entry Strategy

Comprehensive market entry analysis and planning for new markets, customer segments, or geographic expansion.

## Usage

```
/market-entry [target market or segment to enter]
```

Provide details about:
1. What market/segment are you considering entering?
2. What is your current business/product?
3. Why are you considering this market?
4. What resources do you have available?
5. What is your timeline?

## Process

1. **Market Assessment**
   - Market size and growth
   - Customer segments and needs
   - Competitive landscape
   - Regulatory environment

2. **Entry Mode Evaluation**
   - Direct entry options
   - Partnership opportunities
   - Acquisition targets
   - Licensing/franchising

3. **Fit Analysis**
   - Product-market fit requirements
   - Capability gaps
   - Resource requirements

4. **Risk Assessment**
   - Market risks
   - Execution risks
   - Financial risks

5. **Go-to-Market Planning**
   - Entry strategy selection
   - Timeline and milestones
   - Investment requirements

**Output**: Complete market entry analysis with:
- EU market sizing by country
- GDPR and data residency requirements
- Entry mode comparison (direct vs. partnership vs. acquisition)
- Localization requirements (language, payments, support)
- 18-month go-to-market plan starting with UK/Germany
- Investment of $500K with 24-month payback projection
